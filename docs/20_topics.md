# Topic Taxonomy

## Education levels (in scope)

- **ECE** — Early Childhood Education (ages 0–6, including pre-primary)
- **K-12** — Primary and secondary (ages 6–18)

**Higher education is OUT of scope.** Vocational/TVET only if delivered as part of K-12.

## Core themes

These are the editorial through-lines. Every piece should map to at least one. The list is not exhaustive — new themes can be added by the Editor with Manager approval — but it is a strong starting frame.

### Access and equity
- Access to education (geographic, economic, gender, disability)
- Affordability (school fees, materials, opportunity cost)
- Female education and the gender gap
- Education for refugees and displaced children
- Inclusion of children with disabilities

### Identity, language, heritage
- National identity and curriculum
- Preservation of mother-tongue and indigenous languages
- Religious education and respect for religion
- Tolerance, pluralism, intercultural learning
- Heritage and cultural transmission

### The school as a place
- Sustainability and ecological approaches in schools
- Low-technology / no-technology pedagogy
- Anti-bullying and school climate
- Mental health and student wellbeing

### People
- The value and status of teachers and educational professionals
- Parent-led projects and family engagement
- Inspiring individual educators and students
- Community-led schooling

### Systems
- Government-led programs and policy reform
- Public-private partnerships in education
- Successful stories of system change
- Failed reforms and what they taught
- Education ministries and regulators

### Futures
- Preparing students for a world with AI (without uncritical AI cheerleading)
- Future of work and curriculum
- Climate and education

## Themes we avoid

- Edtech product reviews (we're not a buyer's guide)
- US/UK education culture war content
- Pure higher-ed topics
- Personal-development / self-help framings
- Anything that frames children or local educators as needing rescue by outsiders
