# Recon brief — Vietnam makes school tuition-free (2025–26)

**Opened:** 2026-06-12, on the Korea park (see verdict of the same date). **Stage:** source-recon only; no drafting until the index is banked and the two standing gates are named. Per the override-finding order: recon precedes any scope commitment.

## The candidate, in one paragraph

In 2025 Vietnam moved to make public-school tuition free nationwide — the Politburo decision (announced late Feb/early Mar 2025) covering preschool through grade 12, with implementation from the 2025–26 school year. A single-party state choosing, amid a development boom, to remove the fee barrier at every grade: the access story is the obvious read, but the Madār angle is the *fiscal-federal* one — who pays the provinces back, what happens to the "school socialisation" fee culture (the informal-fees problem Vietnamese parents know intimately), and whether free tuition without free *costs* (uniforms, books, "voluntary" contributions) changes enrolment at the margin where it matters.

## Why it fits the catalogue

Completes a Southeast Asia pair with Indonesia (Permen 13 — elective-first AI), and joins the access theme anchored by Bo and Egypt EKB. GCC relevance: several Gulf states run free public schooling for nationals while debating expat access — Vietnam is the natural comparative.

## Tier-1 surfaces to locate (recon to-do)

1. **The Politburo conclusion / Central Committee decision** — the instrument itself (Vietnamese: via baochinhphu.vn, the government portal; English: en.baochinhphu.vn). Date, scope language, grades covered.
2. **MOET implementation guidance** for 2025–26 (moet.gov.vn) — what "tuition" legally covers; what stays charged.
3. **National Assembly budget line** — the compensation mechanism to provinces (state media: VNA/VietnamPlus, VnExpress International datelines).

## The two gates (named now, before any drafting)

1. **Named operational voice:** a serving school head, provincial education-department official, or MOET spokesperson, named, on the record about implementation — not a ministry communiqué.
2. **Figure trace:** the headline fiscal figure (state compensation to provinces, per-year) traced to the Assembly/MOET line — fence against the same-figure-different-scope trap (annual vs multi-year, tuition-only vs full subsidy package).

## Known traps

- "Tuition-free" ≠ "fee-free" — the socialisation-fee culture means informal charges persist; do not let the piece imply otherwise.
- Vietnamese diacritics: names must carry full diacritics in EN (Madār style: render names as their owners write them); AR transliteration protocol runs via the Arabic Editor before any AR ships.
- Implementation is provincial — national announcement ≠ uniform practice; one province's experience is not the country's.
