## What was commissioned

The 2026-05-30 recon memo carried Candidate A forward with one explicit gap: the named-quoted-human apparatus is **one human deep and partly reported speech.** Minister Al-Benyan is quotable at the strategic tier (HCI 2025, verbatim) but the 17 → 90 claim itself reaches us as SPA-relayed reported speech via Argaam, not his verbatim sentence. The memo set the follow-up: *locate one named ECE administrator, kindergarten director, or teacher-training official, quoted verbatim at a primary Arabic-language URL,* with a deadline — *if not located by mid-week, the candidate is parked.*

## What the recon found today

Three search passes, Arabic-language and English:

1. **Arabic — named KG director / administrator statement.** Returned the Ministry kindergarten page, the national registration portal, regional MOE department pages, and the 1447 enrolment announcement. Institutional and procedural; no named operational human with a quote.
2. **Arabic — named teacher, classroom-experience interview.** Returned a university KG-education degree program, the same Ministry pages, and registration guidance. No primary-URL verbatim from a named teacher.
3. **English — named teacher/director interview, "said," Riyadh/Jeddah.** Returned job listings (Bayt, SeekTeachers) and two academic studies (anonymised teacher cohorts). No named individual, no datelined statement.

**Conclusion: the operational-tier voice is not reachable in open primaries at the bar the publication holds.** The strategic tier is well-sourced; the classroom tier is not. That is the exact failure mode the 2026-05-25 discipline tightening exists to catch.

## Decision

**Park Candidate A as an English-reporting piece.** It will not enter the drafts directory. The 17 → 90 arc is a real, dignified, why-now topic, but a piece built only on ministerial reported speech repeats the structural thinness we have already declined twice. We do not draft to a deadline; we draft to a source.

**Hand to the Arabic Editor as an Arabic-original commission.** This is the memo's pre-authorised fallback, and it is the better path, not a consolation. A named kindergarten director or early-childhood supervisor is far more likely to be reachable, on the record, in Arabic-language regional press and Ministry regional channels than in English reporting. An Arabic-original piece — composed, not translated — would also let the publication lead in Arabic for once rather than always translating into it. The Arabic Editor takes the brief: find the operational-tier named human in Arabic primaries; if found, the piece is born Arabic-first with an English companion to follow.
