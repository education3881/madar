## What was commissioned

The 2026-06-03 follow-up parked Candidate A in the English-reporting frame and handed it to the Arabic Editor as the memo's pre-authorised fallback: an **Arabic-original commission**. The brief was specific — find one operational-tier named human (a kindergarten director, an early-childhood supervisor, or a teacher-training official), **quoted verbatim at a primary Arabic-language URL**, with substantive bearing on the 17 → 90 enrolment arc. If found, the piece is composed in Arabic first, English companion to follow. If not, it stays parked.

## What the pass found

Four Arabic-language searches plus targeted fetches:

1. **Named KG director / administrator, verbatim.** The strongest hit is a **Saudi Press Agency** item (a primary, official-record URL): *حصة بنت عبد العزيز الفارس*, **Director of the Kindergarten Department, Riyadh Region**, quoted by name. This clears two of three tests — named human, primary URL — but **fails on date and substance**: it is dated **23 September 2018** (National Day 88), and the quote is ceremonial National-Day register (loyalty, the flag, the founder-king), touching Vision 2030 only in passing. It carries no operational detail on the enrolment arc. A 2018 ceremonial statement is not a why-now operational voice in 2026.

2. **Named early-childhood supervisor (مشرفة الطفولة المبكرة), verbatim, 2024–2026.** Returned the Ministry kindergarten page, the GASTAT *Early Childhood Development & Child Wellbeing Statistics 2025* methodology report, regional MOE department pages, and registration guidance. Institutional and procedural; the supervisory role is described in the abstract, not voiced by a named person.

3. **Named KG teacher, classroom-experience interview, 2025.** Returned a university degree-program page, a teacher's-guide PDF, and generic interview-prep content (TikTok, blogs). No primary-URL verbatim from a named teacher about the rollout.

4. **The 17 → 90 figure attached to a named operational voice, 2025–2026.** Returned the strategic-tier material — the Minister's plan coverage (Sabq, Sayidaty) and the Ministry target language. The figure consistently attaches to the **ministerial / strategic** tier, never to a named classroom or supervisory voice.

## Why this is a park, not a draft

The pattern is now confirmed in **two languages, across two sessions**: the Saudi ECE story is exceptionally well-sourced at the **strategic tier** (Minister Al-Benyan verbatim at HCI 2025; the 17 → 90 target in Ministry and press language) and **not sourced at the operational tier** in open primaries. The single named operational voice that exists on the record (Al-Faris, SPA) is eight years stale and ceremonial. Building the piece on that would repeat exactly the structural thinness the 2026-05-25 discipline tightening exists to catch — a piece about kindergartens with no kindergarten in it.

Arabic did **not** turn out to be the distribution advantage we hoped on this specific topic. The bet was reasonable — operational voices are often more reachable in Arabic regional press than in English reporting — but for Saudi ECE the open-web record at the classroom tier is thin in both languages. That is itself a finding, and it sharpens the commission rather than abandoning it (see the guidebook increment logged today).

## What unparks Candidate A

A single one of the following, and the draft begins the same session:
- A named KG director or early-childhood supervisor, quoted verbatim and **datelined 2024 or later**, at a primary Arabic URL (regional MOE channel, SPA, or a serious Arabic outlet), speaking to the rollout with operational specifics.
- A named KG teacher interviewed on the record in regional Arabic press about the classroom reality of the expansion.
- A **data-led inversion**: if the GASTAT 2025 early-childhood report carries attributable, quotable findings, a single named operational voice could anchor an otherwise data-spine piece — the numbers do the why-now, the human does the dignity. This is the most promising future route and is logged as such.

## Calendar effect

No change to the standing position. The GCC cluster still lacks a Saudi piece; that hole stays **named and honest** in the calendar rather than force-filled. Issue 02's spine (Option B) does not depend on Saudi. The Saudi piece arrives when its source does.
