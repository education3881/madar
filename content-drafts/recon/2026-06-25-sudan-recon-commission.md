# Recon commission — Sudan (founder-requested, 2026-06-25)

**Commissioned by:** Manager, on the founder's prompt ("when are you planning to get something from Sudan?"). **Assigned to:** Researcher. **Target edition:** **03** (post-sprint, slow cadence) — *not* an Edition-02 sprint slot. **Status:** OPEN — recon not yet run; this is the scoping brief, not a source index.

## Why this is a deliberate, slow commission — not a sprint piece

Sudan belongs in the corpus: it is a major Arabic-speaking / African education system, currently absent, and its children are living the largest displacement crisis in the world (civil war since April 2023). But it is **the hardest possible fit for Madār's bar**, and forcing it into the sprint's last two slots would break the rule that kept the bar high. Two structural problems the Researcher must solve *before* any commission:

1. **The tragedy-frame trap.** Sudan's education story is dominated by collapse — schools shut, occupied, or destroyed; ~17–19m children out of school (pre-2025 figures; **re-verify current**). Madār does **not** do the tragedy piece (no morality tale, no savior, no spectacle of suffering). The only Madār-shaped Sudan story is the standing signature: *a system (or a community, or a host state) did an unglamorous, mechanical thing to keep children learning — and someone can show it worked.* If the recon can only find catastrophe, it **parks**, and we say why.
2. **The sourcing bar in a war.** The figure-discipline rulings (#4 + #9–#12) apply *harder* here, not softer: primary surfaces are degraded, numbers are contested, and every actor has a stake. A figure runs only if traced to a named education-sector primary (UN cluster, ministry-in-function, a named evaluator) — never a press round-number from inside the conflict.

## Hard guardrail (non-negotiable — flag for the Editor at commission)

**The education mechanism is the subject; the war is context, never subject.** Madār does **not** enter the conflict's geopolitics — no attribution of atrocities, no taking of sides between SAF and RSF, no commentary on any external state's alleged role. (This guardrail is sharper than usual given the masthead's GCC readership and the founder's own position; the piece must be one an education ministry of *any* alignment could read as fair.) If a candidate angle cannot be told without adjudicating the war, it is the wrong angle.

## Candidate angles to chase (in likely Madār-fit order — verify each gate before banking)

1. **Exam / certification continuity under collapse.** Did Sudan's education authority run, relocate, or adapt the national secondary certificate (Shahada) for displaced students — including Sudanese students sitting exams in Egypt or other host countries? This is the most Madār-shaped: a concrete, measurable, "the system did an unglamorous thing under impossible conditions" story. *Gate:* a named ministry/exam-body operator + a verifiable count of students/centres.
2. **Community-run learning continuity.** The mutual-aid **Emergency Response Rooms** and volunteer/community learning spaces keeping children in some form of schooling. *Gate:* a named local operator (teacher/volunteer coordinator), datelined 2024+, on the *mechanism* — and a figure that isn't self-valorising. *Risk:* tips easily into the savior/tragedy frame; handle only if the measurement holds.
3. **Host-system integration (a Sudan story with a non-Sudan dateline).** How **Chad, South Sudan, or Egypt** absorbed Sudanese refugee children into their schools — a host-state operator + an enrolment figure. Lets us cover Sudan's children through a functioning system rather than a collapsed one; also opens a genuinely new geography (Chad/South Sudan).
4. **Low-tech continuity (radio / offline learning).** Ministry- or agency-run radio or low-bandwidth learning for out-of-school children. *Gate:* named operator + verifiable reach + (ideally) any learning measurement.

## Definition of done for the recon
A banked source index (Section 1 format) that either: **(a) PROCEED** — one angle with the figure gate closed to a named primary and a named operational voice (2024+) on the mechanism, the tragedy-frame avoidable in print; or **(b) PARK** — a written statement of which gate could not close and the single condition that would un-park it. **Either outcome is a success**; a park here is the bar working, exactly as Saudi/Jordan parked.
