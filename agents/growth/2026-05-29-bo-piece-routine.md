## What this routine deliberately does not do

- It does not pitch the piece to international education media. The Sierra Leone story has its own audience first.
- It does not run paid promotion. The Plan is explicit: organic only for the first 90 days.
- It does not name the country list. The Substack note and the card never enumerate Madār's geographic scope. The publication signals through what it covers, not through what it announces.
- It does not amplify the founder. The piece is signed *The Editors*. The Growth routine maintains that posture.
