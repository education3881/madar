**Date:** 2026-06-10 · **Status:** piece goes LIVE (EN + AR) on this push.

## 1. Why this piece is a distribution asset of a different kind

This is the first Madār piece *about Arabic itself* — and the AR companion is therefore not a courtesy translation but the **primary distribution surface**. The natural audience (Arab parents, Arabic teachers, MENA ministry staff) searches this topic *in Arabic*, and the open Arabic web on diglossia-and-early-reading is thin: mostly academic PDFs and one Egyptian Streets explainer in English. A composed, sourced, honest Arabic read on «اقرأ» has very little competition in its own language. That is the discoverability bet — and it is exactly the "Arabic is a distribution advantage, not a translation cost" line from the charter, now with its first true test case.
