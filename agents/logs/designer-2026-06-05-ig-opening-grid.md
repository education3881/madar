## Rendering note

The seven exports are the published stills (each `viewBox 1200×600`, pure line-art — no fonts) rendered at 1080×540 and composited centred on a 1080×1080 paper (`#FAFAF7`) canvas, padded above and below rather than cropped. This honours the brief's instruction to **pad with paper, not scale to bleed** — and it gives the grid a consistent, airy editorial margin that reads as one hand at thumbnail size. Tile 5 sources the canonical path-based wordmark (`/design-assets/wordmark/madar-wordmark.svg`), so the mark *is* the typeface, not an approximation. Tile 8 is pure geometry in the site's `--color-rule` ink and `#D94F2A` orange.

## Flag for a second look (Growth's call, not a blocker)

The still tiles carry generous top/bottom paper margins because the source stills are 2:1, not square. Verified on a 3×3 contact sheet: the grid is coherent and on-brand.
