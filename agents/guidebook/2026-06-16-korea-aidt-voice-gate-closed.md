**Subject:** South Korea's statutory reversal of AI Digital Textbooks (AIDT) — adopted with fanfare for 2025, then downgraded from official "textbook" to optional "educational material."

## The reversal (primary, verified 2026-06-16)
- National Assembly amendment **passed 4 Aug 2025** narrows the legal definition of "textbook" to printed/e-books, excluding "learning support software using intelligent information technology" — i.e. AIDT loses textbook status; effective on promulgation. The **second attempt** (an identical 2024 bill was vetoed by then-acting-president Choi Sang-mok); passed after Yoon's removal and Lee Jae-myung's election (Lee campaigned to rescind AIDT).
  - Korea Herald, "South Korea pulls plug on AI textbooks," 4 Aug 2025 — https://www.koreaherald.com/article/10546695 (fetched)
  - MLex corroboration — https://www.mlex.com/mlex/articles/2373192
- **2026 status:** publishers filed an administrative damages suit (Apr 2025) and a **constitutional complaint (Dec 2025)** with the Constitutional Court; **no ruling as of mid-2026 — state as PENDING, do not imply finality.** Adoption fell ~37% (1st sem) → ~19% (Sept 2025), ~2,095 schools.
