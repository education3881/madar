# The publishable-subset rule — publish what you can read, name what you can't (Oman)

**Banked:** 2026-06-20 · **Owner:** Researcher / Editor · **Type:** durable ruling (#11) + two companion frames
**Surfaced by:** Oman school-performance ratings (sprint piece 7). The recon banked this candidate as **CONDITIONAL** — figure gate OPEN, voice gate GAP — and it shipped anyway, cleanly, by the discipline below. This note is how a conditional candidate closes without forcing or fabricating.

---

## Ruling #11 — the publishable-subset rule (the fifth dimension of figure discipline #4)

**When the primary surface that would settle a figure is unreachable *and* the second-hand tallies do not reconcile, do not publish the disputed number. Publish only the subset that triangulates from reachable primaries, and state the withholding in print.**

Oman's case, exactly:
- The per-band tally relayed in the press — 44 *suitable* / 3 *good* / 11 *unsuitable* / 1 *needs rapid intervention* — **sums to 59, not 58**, and "44 suitable" collides with "44 government." Internally inconsistent at the secondary tier.
- The surface that would settle it — the OAAAQA public portal (`oaaaqa.gov.om`) — is **unreachable from the sandbox** (egress wall; and a dynamic portal even if reached). So the figure cannot be re-derived to primary.
- **Therefore the band tally does not run at all.** What *does* run is the subset that triangulates across reachable primaries and is internally consistent: the **first-phase reach (58 government + private schools)** and the **single school placed at the bottom band (al-Hassan bin Hashim, Seeb)** — both corroborated across Times of Oman, Muscat Daily, Oman Observer and Atheer.
- **And the piece says so:** *"the band-by-band tallies relayed second-hand do not yet reconcile, so we have reported only what can be read directly … and left the disputed breakdown for the portal to settle."*

Why the disclosure is a feature, not an apology: on a story whose **entire merit is that a state chose to show its work**, visibly refusing to round or guess a number *is* the credibility signal — it demonstrates the discipline the piece is praising. The reader trusts the 58 and the one named school precisely because we declined the shaky 59.

**Verdict-checklist line:** *Figure surface: ☐ primary reachable & consistent → publish · ☐ primary unreachable OR secondary inconsistent → publish only the triangulated subset, state the withholding · ☐ neither subset nor disclosure possible → hold.*

This is distinct from and compounds with the prior dimensions of #4: **#4** read-it-right · **#9** keep-it-in-currency (don't sum across reporting bodies) · **#10** right-instrument (don't let a real number adjudicate what it can't) · **#11** publish-only-what-you-can-read (don't assert what the primary won't confirm). A figure must clear all four to carry weight.

## Companion frame A — the "adopt-as-the-model-retreats" timing check (reusable comparative framing)

When a country **imports a foreign instrument**, check the instrument's *current status in its country of origin* before framing the import as simple progress or simple folly. The import may be arriving **as the source reforms or abandons it.** Oman built an Ofsted-style published-single-verdict inspectorate in 2024–26; England scrapped one-word Ofsted judgements in **September 2024** (after the Ruth Perry coroner finding) and moved to multi-dimension report cards. That timing *is* the piece — neither "Gulf modernises" nor "Gulf copies a bad idea," but the sharper question of whether the importer inherits the **fix** or only the **mechanism**. Run this check on any "country X adopts Y's model" candidate: *what is Y doing with that model right now?*

## Companion frame B — how a CONDITIONAL candidate closes (route-2 closure pattern)

A candidate banked with **figure OPEN + voice GAP** does not have to slip or be forced. It can ship as an **explicit systems/transparency piece** when, and only when, both gaps are handled honestly *in the piece itself*:
1. **Figure:** the disputed number is **withheld**, not asserted (ruling #11); only the triangulated subset runs; the withholding is stated.
2. **Voice:** the operational-voice gap is **named in print** ("we have the authority's voice … not yet the principals and teachers inside the named schools"); no fabricated operator quote; the CEO is labelled as the authority tier, not smuggled in as the operator.
3. It is the **Editor's call**, on the record (here: verdict `2026-06-20`, both gates marked *discharged via route 2*), with the precedent named (Atheer's own March systems-analysis carried no operator voice either).

This keeps **quality-over-slot** and **slot-kept** from being in tension: the bar does not move (nothing unverified ships), and the day still advances (the conditional piece closes on honest terms rather than waiting on a portal we cannot reach). The upgrade path is logged: a named serving principal/governorate supervisor quoted 2024+ lifts this from curated systems-piece to original reporting.
