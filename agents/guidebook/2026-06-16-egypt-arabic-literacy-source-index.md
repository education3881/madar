# SOURCE INDEX — Egypt: Arabic diglossia & early literacy (NPDA)

**Compiled:** 2026-06-16 (recon pass) · **Status: PROCEED / commission-ready.** · **Region: MENA**
**Subject:** Egypt's National Programme for Developing Arabic Language Skills (NPDA) as the scaled answer to a diglossia-shadowed early-reading crisis — the same spoken-vs-written-Arabic gap as our Ras Al Khaimah "Iqra" piece, but at ~100-million-speaker scale no emirate can model.

## Why now (2024–2026 hook)
NPDA — a 60-hour remedial Arabic-literacy programme — launched **Feb 2025** (Ministry of Education and Technical Education + UNICEF + German Cooperation/KfW). A **UNICEF Innocenti implementation-research report (Sept 2025)** carries first-phase results; Phase Two and a **25 Mar 2026** UNICEF Egypt feature keep it live.
