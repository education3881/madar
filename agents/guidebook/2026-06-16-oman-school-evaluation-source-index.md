# SOURCE INDEX — Oman: National System for Evaluating School Performance

**Compiled:** 2026-06-16 · **Status: PROCEED — with two conditions (below).** · **Region: MENA (GCC — the one GCC state Madār has not yet covered).**
**Subject:** Oman's England-Ofsted-style external school-inspection regime — and the rare GCC act of **publicly naming individual government schools as underperforming.**

## Why now (sharp, fresh hook)
Not an announcement — a **closed first cycle.** Launched 2 Dec 2024; first evaluation phase from Sept 2025; **public per-school ratings began 7 Jan 2026**; by mid-March 2026, 58 schools had graded results on the authority's public portal, including government schools rated "unsuitable" / "needs rapid intervention."

## Instruments / sources (verified to resolve 2026-06-16)
- Issuing body: **Oman Authority for Academic Accreditation and Quality Assurance of Education (OAAAQA)** — renamed by **Royal Decree 12/2026** to the *Oman Authority for Quality Assurance of Education* (a 2026 line worth noting).
- Anchored in the School Education Law (**Royal Decree 31/2023**) + National Strategy for Education 2040 + Vision 2040.
- Launch (Times of Oman/ONA): timesofoman.com/article/152660-...
- Phase-1 launch + CEO interview (Muscat Daily, 14 Sep 2025): muscatdaily.com/2025/09/14/oman-to-launch-first-phase-of-school-performance-evaluation-today/
- Results announcement (Times of Oman, 5 Jan 2026): timesofoman.com/article/166925-...
- Results analysis w/ named schools (Atheer, 16 Mar 2026): atheer.om/2026/03/16/102944
- **Primary results portal:** oaaaqa.gov.om/public/external-activities/cseqa — **verify on open before publishing any counts.**
